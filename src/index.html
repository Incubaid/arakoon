Arakoon

is a distributed key-value store that guarantees consistency above anything else. It was created by the Incubaid team due to a lack of existing solutions fitting our requirements, and is available as Open Source software.

It provides

  1. Consistency
  2. Conditional & atomic updates
  3. Robustness
  4. Locality control
  5. Healing & recovery
  6. Explicit failure
